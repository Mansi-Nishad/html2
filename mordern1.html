What is HTML

HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) is the standard markup language used to create and structure content on the web. It is the backbone of web pages and is essential for building the structure of documents, including text, images, links, forms, and more.

What are Semantic Tags

Semantic tags in HTML provide meaning and structure to content. They convey the intended purpose of the content, making it more understandable for browsers, developers, and assistive technologies. Examples of semantic tags include <header>, <nav>, <article>, <section>, <footer>, and more.
